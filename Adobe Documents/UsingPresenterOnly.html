Using the Adobe Connect Presenter Only Area

There is an area of Adobe Connect known as the Presenter Only area. When turned on, the Presenter Only area appears on the right side of the meeting room and has a black background (as compared to the gray background of the stage). Any pods placed in the Adobe Presenter area are visible to meeting presenters and hosts, but not visible to participants.

 

Enable the Presenter Only Area

To turn on the Presenter Only Area

1.   Click the Meeting menu from the toolbar at the top of the window

2.   Select the Enable Presenter Only Area option (click the option again to turn off the Presenter Only area

 

Moving Pods into and out of the Presenter Only Area

When the Presenter Only area is turned on, all new pods that are opened will automatically be placed into that area.

You can move pods from within the meeting room "stage" by simply dragging them into the Presenter Only area.

You can also move pods from the Presenter Only area to the stage by dragging and dropping them.
