Sharing content in the Share pod

The Share pod controls let Hosts and Presenters share content in various ways. You can maximize the pod to make contents larger.

When you share content in the Share pod all activities you perform in a shared window, application, or document are visible to attendees. The file formats supported in the Share pod are JPG, PNG, SWF, PPT, PPTX, PDF, FLV, F4V, MP3, MP4, and ZIP.

How to Share content

  • Select the “Pods” tab, share, and either click “Add New Share” or select one of the pods listed in the red textbox below (example only) that are already uploaded into your meeting.
