Users with Multiple Logins

 

Quite often there are cases where attendees may need to navigate away from the Adobe Connect meeting room to search the web or use a different application during a class.

 

This is perfectly fine, however, there are many instances when attendees do this and are later unable to locate their meeting room, so they log back in through their internet browser. The issue with this is that it logs the individual in twice and in turn causes their audio to echo.

 

To prevent this, either minimize the meeting window before navigating outside of it, or minimize your web browser and all other applications until you find the meeting room, or you can click on the Adobe Connect icon in your dock bar at the bottom of your screen.

 

 

 

NOTE: Adobe Connect is a separate application from your web browser (Firefox specifically).
