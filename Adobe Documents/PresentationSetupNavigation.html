Presentation Setup and Navigation

 

Setup

 

In order to avoid issues with presentations please follow these directions before submitting presentations for upload into Adobe Connect:

 1.   Create a slideshow using any program that can convert slides to a PDF format (e.g., PowerPoint, Keynote, Google Slides*, etc.). If you do not convert to the PDF format, there may be problems with how your slides appear in Adobe Connect.

2.   Do not use animations or special effects of any kind in your slides. Please only upload one file and keep it under 10MG

3.   Save your slideshow in the PDF format.

4.   Review the saved PDF to make sure all your formatting is correct.

5.   Adjust any slides with bad formatting in your slideshow program and resave as a PDF.

6.   Repeats steps 4 and 5 until you are satisfied.

 

 

 

 

 

 

*If you use Google slides, be sure to adjust the page setup to 4:3 by going to File > Page setup… and selecting 4:3 from the Page setup dropdown menu that pops up before exporting as a PDF.

 

 

Presentation toolbar options

 

You can control the presentation appearance and playback by using the toolbar located at the bottom of the presentation.

Up Arrow

Move to the previous slide in the presentation.

Down Arrow

Move to the next slide in the presentation.

Current Slide Number

Shows the number of the currently displayed slide and the total number of slides (for example, 2/10).

Plus/Minus

         Allow you to zoom in/out on slides

Counter Clockwise Arrow

         Rotate the presentation counter clockwise

Clockwise Arrow

         Rotate the presentation clockwise

Fit to Width

         Maximizes width of presentation

Fit Page

Maximizes entire page to fit perfectly in the Share Pod
