How to Set Adobe Meeting Room to Full Screen

 

Adjust Adobe Connect Share Pod View (Full Screen, Scale to Fit and Zoom)

When you are viewing an Adobe Connect session as a participant, you can adjust the size of the image you see from the Host’s shared desktop or application.

When you are in an Adobe Connect meeting and a host or presenter is sharing their desktop or an application, you have some options that will control how large their shared content will appear to you.

  •  Scale to Fit – This view will display the entire screen image within the Share Pod. With this view you can watch the screen; then comment or ask questions in the Chat Pod. However, depending on the resolution of your monitor the ‘Scale to Fit’ button may be hard to read.

 

 

Resuming Normal View: Hover your mouse over to the top right of the window until the “Scale to Fit” icon appears then click on it
