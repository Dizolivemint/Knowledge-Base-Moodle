How to Resubmit or Change an Assignment

 

Most assignments will only allow one submission.

Submitting assignments is a two step process.
1. Upload your assignment and click Save Changes. This puts the assignment in a "Draft, not Submitted" status.
2. Click the Submit Assignment" button to submit the assignment for grading.

You CAN make changes or resubmit an assignment while it is in "Draft" status.
After you click "Submit Assignment" you will no longer be able to make any changes to the assignment.

 

 

 

1.  Contact your instructor and explain your reasons.

 

To resubmit an assignment, you will need your instructor's permission. Depending on the due date and the reason, the instructor may elect to not allow an assignment to be resubmitted.

Your instructor will need to change the status of the assignment from "Submitted for grading" back to a "Draft, not submitted" status.

 

 

 

2.  Delete the erroneous assignment

 

 

After the instructor has changed the status back to a "Draft, not submitted" status, open the assignment page and click the "Edit submission" button. The dropbox page will open with your document included.

Click on the document icon in the dropbox window. Click "Delete." This will remove the assignment you want to replace.

 

 

 

3.  Upload the replacement assignment

 

 

With the old assignment deleted, you can now upload your new assignment document by dropping it into the dropbox window.

Click Save changes when finished. The assignment page will display and show your new assignment in a "Draft, not submitted" status.

 

 

 

4.  Click Submit Assignment

 

 

Click the Submit assignment button to change the updated assignment to a "Submitted for grading" status. You will not be able to make any further changes once the assignment has been submitted for grading.
