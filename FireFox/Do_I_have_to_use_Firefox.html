Do I have to use Firefox?

 

 

The Firefox internet browser is the recommended browser for use with the Pacific College eLearning system. All aspects of the eLearning system have been tested and are known to work properly using Firefox.

Several minor issues have been known to occur when using other internet browsers, such as Safari, Internet Explorer, and Chrome. If you report issues to the Help Desk and need assistance, you will be first asked if you are using Firefox,

 

 

1.  What about mobile devices? Do I need Firefox on my phone?

 

 

The mobile version of Pacific College eLearning works just fine in Safari on iPhones or other browsers on non-Apple mobile phones. The mobile version of Pacific College eLearning opens automatically when accessed via a mobile phone, but you will likely view the full website when accessing via a tablet or iPad device.

 

 

 

2.  Where can I get Firefox?

 

 

You can download Firefox.com or from Mozilla.org (Mozilla is the company that built the Firefox product). Firefox is free and also one of the most secure internet browsers available.

Be wary of downloading Firefox from any other websites that may come up in a Google or Bing search, as downloads from sites other than Firefox.com or Mozilla.org may also include unwanted software and possible viruses.
