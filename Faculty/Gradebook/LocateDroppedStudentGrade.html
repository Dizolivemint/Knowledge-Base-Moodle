How to Find a Dropped Student's Grade

 

By default, students who have dropped a class are hidden from the gradebook. However, in some cases a teacher or administrator may want to retrieve those grades. A common example is when a student has changed to a new section of the same class and it is necessary to reenter the grades in the gradebook of the new section.

To access a dropped student's grades, do the following:

1. Go to Settings > Grades.
2. Select the "My preferences" tab.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
4. Change the setting named "Show only active enrollments" to "No."
5. Return to the Grader report to access the dropped student's grades.

Note: This setting change will affect all classes you teach. So, you may want to change it back after you have accessed the necessary information.

You are welcome to leave the grades of dropped students visible, just be aware that if you accidentally enter a grade for a dropped student they will receive an alert. Dropped students are shown in the gradebook with a flag next to their name and the attendance report indicates the date they dropped the class.
