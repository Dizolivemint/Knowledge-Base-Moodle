Regrading a Quiz

There are times you may need to throw out a question or allow other answers after the quiz has been graded.

1.    Click the Quiz name in the course shell. Click Results > Grades in the Settings block.

2.    For each individual student, click the missed question.

 

3.    Click "Make Comment or Override".

 

4.    Make a comment and change the mark.

 

5.    Click "Save"

 

6.    Close the pop-up window after saving.

 

7.    Click Regrade all to apply the changes.

 

8.    Check to see that the grade has changed for all questions.
