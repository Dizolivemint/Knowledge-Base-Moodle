Quizzes: How to Delete a Quiz Attempt

 

 

.

1. Click the quiz name on the course homepage.

 

 

 

2. Click Attempts on the quiz page that opens up.

 

 

 

3. On the Attempts page, put a check next to each attempt you want delete.

 

 

 

 

 

4. At the bottom of the page, click the Delete selected attempts button.
