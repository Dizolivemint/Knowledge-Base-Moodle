Review Quiz Attempts and Quiz Statistics

 

One of the great things about online quizzes is that you have access to immediate statistics. You can review how long it takes students to take the quiz, when they log in and make their attempt, and review which questions they are answering correctly and which ones they may be consistently getting wrong.

 

1.  Open the Quiz

Click on the link to open the quiz or exam for which you wish to review.

 

2.  Click on the "Attempts" link

The Attempts link will have a number next to it that represents the number of students that have attempted the quiz. It is located just above the "Attempt Quiz Now" button towards the bottom of the page. This button may also say "Preview Quiz Now." The Attempts screen will display with the quiz statistics

 

3.  Review individual quiz statistics

This screen will display all students who have attempted to take the quiz. It will also show when they logged in, when they finished, the total time taken to complete, the total score, and the score for each question answered.

 

4.  Delete an attempt

From this page, you can delete an attempt (as if the student never took the quiz) by selecting the checkbox to the left of their name. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on the "Delete Selected Attempts" button to remove the attempt. Please note that rather than deleting the attempt, you may also override specific students attempts through the Quiz Administration menu. Please see the "Override Individual Quiz Settings" article for instructions on that process.
