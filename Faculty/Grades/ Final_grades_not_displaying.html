eLearning Gradebook: Final grades not displaying

 

Category totals and the overall course total will not calculate and display if there are missing items or holes in the gradebook. Below are some tips for entering grades to ensure that category totals and the overall course total are calculated correctly.

 

 

 

 

1.  Enter "zero" as a score for items that were not turned in.

 

 

If a student fails to submit an assignment and will receive zero points for the assignment, the instructor must manually enter a zero into the gradebook field. A field that is left blank is NOT automatically assumed to be a zero score.

Blank fields essentially have a null value until a value is entered. Null fields are not included in the overall course or category totals.

 

 

 

2.   Eliminating existing assignments from the gradebook:

 

 

Example: There is a Unit 1 Quiz, which the instructor chooses to toss out because the text which questions were based was not available at the beginning of the term.

The instructor must delete the quiz from the course to eliminate the item from the gradebook. Turn the editing mode on and then click the red X next to the Unit 1 Quiz to delete the item from the class.

 

 

 

3.  Hidden items still remain in the grade book.

 

 

If you hide an assignment or quiz (by clicking the eye icon so it shows as a closed eyelid with editing mode turned on), the graded item will remain in the grade book. If no grades are entered in the gradebook for that assignment or quiz, category totals and an overall course grade cannot be calculated until the null fields have a value entered in them.

If the instructor intends to toss out the assignment or quiz and not include it in the overall course grade, the item must be deleted from the gradebook as if it never existed.

 

 

 

4.  Eliminate a graded item for an individual student.

 

 

You can exclude a graded item from the category total and overall course grade for an individual student (and keep the assignment for the rest of the class)

- Select Settings > Grades, to view the Grader Report page.
- Select Turn Editing On button in the upper right corner (a yellow "edit" pencil will appear next to every field)
- Locate the field for the assignment and student name you wish to edit. Click the yellow pencil next to that specific field.
- Check the "exclude" box to designate that assignment as excluded for that student.
- Click the Save Changes button.
- You will now see a red "Excluded" label in the field, indicating that the assignment has been removed from the aggregation for that student.
