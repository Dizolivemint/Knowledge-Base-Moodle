Taking Attendance in Campus Vue Portal

Attendance records are now located in the Campus Vue Portal.

To enter or review attendance records go to https://portal.pacificcollege.edu/. You will need to log in using your Campus Vue credentials which may be different from the credentials you use to access other college systems.

Click the link below to watch a tutorial on taking attendance in Campus Vue http://elearning.pacificcollege.edu/mod/page/view.php?id=105179
