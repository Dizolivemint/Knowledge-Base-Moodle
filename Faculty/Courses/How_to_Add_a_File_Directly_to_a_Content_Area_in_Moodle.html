How to Add a File Directly to a Content Area in Moodle

 

 

 

This is the recommended way to add files to Moodle. This method allows you to organize your files in a variety of different ways. For example, you can add files to content areas organized by the week in which they are used, by topic, alphabetically, by assignment, etc. and have them appear right on your course landing page. This makes it easy for students to find.
