How to Hide & Unhide Items on the Course Home Page

 

Instructors have the ability to hide or unhide items on the Course Home Page to make them visible or not visible to the students enrolled in the class. For example, if the instructor does not plan to use the "Question and Comments forum" to communicate with students through the eLearning interface the item can be hidden on the page so that students do not see it and are not confused by it's presence on the page.

 

1.   In the Course Home Page click “Turn editing on”.

Click the "Turn editing on" button located in the upper right corner of the page.

 

 

2.   You will need to click on the Edit drop down right of the course information, activity, or assignment.

 

 

3.    To hide an item click the word hide with the eye icon.

 


The text will turn gray indicating that the item is hidden from student view. As an instructor with instructor access rights in the system you will still be able to view the item. However, the student will not.

4.   Turn editing off

After hiding the items you wish to hide click the "Turn editing off" button to make the editing icons disappear.

 

5.   Unhide an item and make it visible again.

You will want to choose “Show” from the “Edit” drop down and turn editing on. Once an item is hidden the text will be gray. To unhide the item and make it visible to students again simply click the closed eye icon and it will reopen and the text will show in color.
