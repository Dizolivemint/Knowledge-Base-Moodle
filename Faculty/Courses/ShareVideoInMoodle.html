How to Share a Video

File uploads on Pacific College eLearning are limited to 10 MB. Large video files are usually much larger than this. So, they need to be uploaded to an external site like YouTube, Vimeo, Google Drive, Dropbox, or Screencast. Below are references to further help using these services at the bottom of the page.
