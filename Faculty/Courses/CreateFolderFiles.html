How to Create a Folder and Add Files

1.   Select “Turn on editing” in the upper right hand side of the course home page.


 

 

2.   Click the “+Add an activity or resource” and a popup window will open.

 

 

3.   Check the “Folder” button and click the “Add” button.


 

 

4. Appropriately name the folder.

 


5. Click the Save and display button

 


6. Proceed to add your files by either dragging and dropping or browsing for them.
