Add a File Directly to Content Area in Moodle

 

This is the recommended way to add files to Moodle. This method allows you to organize your files in a variety of different ways. For example, you can add files to content areas organized by the week in which they are used by topic, alphabetically, assignment, etc. and have them appear right in your course home page.
