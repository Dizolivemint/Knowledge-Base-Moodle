Naming Sections in the Course Home Page

 

The default course name in each course shell is "Enter Course Name Here" it is easy to personalize the Course Home Page with the title of the course.

 

1.  From the Course Home Page, turn editing on.

Click the "Turn editing on" button, located in the upper right corner of the page. The small editing icons next to each course activity will display.

 

 

2.  Under the tab with "Enter Course Name Here" click the small pencil icon.

The yellow pencil icon is located just below the title tab on the left side. (See illustration to the right to see exactly where it is located.)
The editing page will open.

 

 

3.  On the editing page enter the course title.

In the “Section Name field” enter the title of the course. The course title should match the course title listed in the Pacific College Course Catalog. Do not use any abbreviations. This will help eliminate any confusion when students log into the course for the first time and want to verify they are viewing the correct course. You can also add "Welcome to..." at the beginning of the course title if you like. For example, "Welcome to Introduction to Tui Na Hand Techniques".

 

4.  Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Changes.

To save the new course title click the “Save Changes” button at the bottom of the page.

 

 

5.  Turn editing off.

Back on the “Course Home Page” you will now see the course title in place of “Enter Course Name Here”. Click the “Turn editing off” button in the upper right corner to remove the editing icons from the page.
