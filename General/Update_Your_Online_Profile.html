Update Your Online Profile

Follow the directions below to update your profile with a brief bio and headshot.

If you prefer a short video, click here.

 

1.    Click on your name on the upper right-hand side of any page.

 

 

 

2.    When it opens click on My Profile.

 

 

 

3.    Click on Edit profile under User Details.

 

 

4.    Do the following:

1.     Change the Timezone setting to your local area.

2.     Add a brief bio to the Description field.

3.     Add a photo of yourself in the User picture area.
