Set the Time Zone in Pacific College eLearning

 

The default setting for the time zone in the Pacific College eLearning is Pacific Time (America-Los Angeles).

If you live in a different time zone, you will need to adjust your time settings by following the directions below:

 

 

1.    From the My Classes page, Choose Settings > Edit Profile

 

 

In the Settings menu, under the My Profile Settings header, find the Edit Profile link. See image to the right for a visual aid.

If you have not moved your settings menu to the left side bar, you will find the Settings menu at the top of the column of feature blocks along the right side of the My Classes page.

 

 

 

 

2.  On the Edit Profile page, locate Timezone

 

 

The timezone setting is located towards the bottom of the General settings, just up from the Description field. See image to the right for a visual aid.

 

 

 

3.  Select your own timezone from the list.

 

 

 

4.  Click Save Changes

 

 

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on Save changes.
