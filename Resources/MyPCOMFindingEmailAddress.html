MyPCOM: Finding an Email Address

1.   Log in to MyPCOM

2. Select "Personal Tools" from the MyPCOM dropdown menu

3. Click on "Address Book"

4. Once you are in the Address Book, click "Add From Directory"

Once you do this you can search by name for student or faculty.

 

 

 

Office 365 Can Find Email Address

 

1.   Log onto your Office 365 using your username and password.

2.   Click on People Tile

 

 

 

3.     Can Type in the Search People to find a particular name, or you click on Directory and see everyone’s name
